The focal filesystem, referenced by file://, is treated as a hostile Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) environment. AJAX now entirely fails on Google Chrome, with urls starting with "file://". You are now forced to start up a web server on your local system, and use http://127.0.0.1/. Good Luck. Raise a bugreport with google chrome if you don't like what google have done, but please do not raise one with pyjamas: it is not our problem.
